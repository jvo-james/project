Case Study: Vitra AI — African Digital Innovation Challenge

Problem • Approach • Impact

Challenge

Clinics in resource-constrained areas need quick, interpretable decision support for common conditions. Existing systems are either black-box or heavy and expensive to deploy.

My role

I led the product design and technical implementation: data pipeline, model training & evaluation, explainability layer, and a responsive front-end for clinicians and patients.

Approach

  • Built lightweight ML models focused on recall for early detection.
  • Implemented SHAP-style explainability to highlight contributing factors.
  • Designed a mobile-first UI with clinician review flow and low-bandwidth fallbacks.
  • Packaged services with Docker and deployed demo API endpoints for testing.

Impact

Winner — African Digital Innovation Challenge. Positive clinician feedback on interpretability and the potential to reduce unnecessary referrals in early pilots.

Technical summary

  1. Frontend: React + accessible components, progressive enhancement
  2. Backend: FastAPI for model serving and secure audit logging
  3. ML: TensorFlow models with confidence estimates and local explainability
  4. Infra: Docker containers, lightweight hosting for low-cost deployment
