ABOUT THE CREATOR

I have been developing NFTs since 2020, and the problem is often that the supply is too high and the developers just want to make a lot of money with a mint and then let the project die. With a 10,000 NFT collection, one costs at least $10 = $100,000 mint reward. My NFT collection consists of 111 NFTs, and I am trying to create utilities and airdrops worth a total of $10,000+ for Dralien Mainnet holders. 10,000/111 = ~$100 per NFT. This ensures that WL holders have a secure, good investment.

The goal: a compact, utility-first drop that rewards holders over time rather than a one-off mint pump. Monthly airdrops, physical rewards, and community-first access are core to that mission.
